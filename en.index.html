A Mechanical engine

This webpage was made for the course Manufacturing Processes (Framleiðsluferli) at the University of Iceland. The objective of this course was to make the students aware of different manufacturing processes, help them to choose between different processes, given their pros and cons. An insight is given into production of finished goods from raw materials with different processes, e.g. moulding, forming, extrusion, machining and cutting processes. Permanent and non-permanent methods of assembling parts are explained along with different methods of surface treatments for strengthening and protecting against the environment. A practical assignment connects the course material with real time projects in metalworking.
